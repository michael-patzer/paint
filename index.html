Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Paint Color Match & Preview

How accurate is the color matching?

Our AI-powered color matching technology is highly accurate, with up to 90%+ precision in most lighting conditions. For best results, take photos in natural daylight and ensure proper focus. The app works completely offline, so you can match colors anywhere.

Which paint brands are included in the app?

We include colors from all major paint manufacturers including Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, Behr, Valspar, PPG, Pantone, RAL, Vista, Avery, Dunn-Edwards, Dutch Boy, IKEA, & Farrow-Ball. Our database is regularly updated with the latest colors.

Can I share paint colors with others?

Yes! You can easily share your saved or favorite paint colors via email, text, or social media directly from the app. This is perfect for getting feedback from family members or collaborating with designers and contractors.
